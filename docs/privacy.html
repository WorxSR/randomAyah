Privacy Policy for Quran Extension

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how the Quran Extension ("the Extension") handles user data. This extension is available on the Chrome Web Store and is designed to fetch and display random Quranic verses (ayahs) using the Alquran.cloud public API.

1. Data Collection

The Quran Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) or user-specific data. We do not use tracking technologies, cookies, or analytics services.
